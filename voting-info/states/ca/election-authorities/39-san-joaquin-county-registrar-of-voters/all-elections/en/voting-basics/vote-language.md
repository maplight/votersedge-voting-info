title: "How do I vote in San Joaquin County if I speak a language other than English?"
published: true
weight: 12
section: "voting-basics"

---

The San Joaquin County Registrar of Voters provides all election materials, including ballots, in Español/Spanish. For voting information en Español call (209) 953-1052.

Voters have the right to request a translated sample ("facsimile") ballot in the following languages: 
- 中文/Chinese
- Tagalog/Filipino
- ខ្មែរ/Khmer
- Punjabi
- Tiếng Việt/Vietnamese
- हिन्दी/Hindi
- Hmong
- ລາວ/Laotian 
- اردو/Urdu 

 Facsimile ballots and polling place information may be available at some selected precincts. Contact the Registrar of Voters at (209) 468-VOTE (8683) or [by email](mailto:vbm@sjgov.org) to receive a facsimile ballot by mail OR to receive a list of in-person locations with facsimile ballots.
