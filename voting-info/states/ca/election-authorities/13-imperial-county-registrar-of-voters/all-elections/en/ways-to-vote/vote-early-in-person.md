**You can vote in person starting before Election Day from Monday, October 10 through Election Day, Tuesday, November 8.**  

**Early voting location and hours:**  
Imperial County Elections Office  
940 W. Main Street, Suite 206  
El Centro, CA 92243  
During business hours from Mondays through Fridays, October 10 through November 8, 8 AM to 5 PM.
