Check out this great opportunity to help your community while earning extra money: be a Poll Worker in [Sacramento County](http://www.elections.saccounty.net/Pages/Work-the-Election.aspx)!

- Chinese 
- Vietnamese

- Hindi 
- Korean 
- Japanese

- Hmong
